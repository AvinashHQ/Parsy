# Operator Interview Screener

Assign an anonymized candidate ID before recording responses.

## Qualification questions

1. What is your current role?
2. Do you personally enter, validate, review or supervise supplier invoice/receipt data?
3. Approximately how many supplier invoices or receipts does your team process each month?
4. What percentage do you personally handle or review?
5. Which input types are common: digital PDF, scan, photo, email body, spreadsheet, XML or hybrid PDF/XML?
6. Do documents involve multiple supplier countries, currencies or languages?
7. Do you enter line items, only header/totals, or both?
8. What is the final destination: spreadsheet, Tally, QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, NetSuite, SAP, another ERP, or manual journal entry?
9. Can you walk through a representative recent batch without sharing documents outside your approved environment?
10. Are you open to a 45–60 minute research call?

## Qualify when

- The participant personally performs or reviews the workflow.
- They can describe a recent real process at field level.
- The workflow includes manual entry, review or reconciliation.
- They agree to anonymous operational notes.

## Strong-priority signals

- 500+ documents per month.
- Multiple supplier layouts.
- Multi-currency or multi-country invoices.
- Recurring import-template preparation.
- Line-item entry.
- High correction or client-query burden.
- Willingness to demonstrate the workflow.

## Exclude or deprioritize when

- They cannot describe the current process beyond management-level summaries.
- Their workflow is already fully structured and touchless.
- They are primarily trying to sell a competing product or consultancy.
- Participation requires copying sensitive documents into unapproved tools.
