# Consent and Privacy Script

## Opening

Thank you for joining. This is a workflow-research session, not a product demo or tax/compliance assessment. I want to understand how supplier invoices move through your current process, where time is spent and what kinds of errors or exceptions occur.

Please do not disclose information you are not authorized to share. You may use a redacted, synthetic or representative document. I do not need a copy of the document for this interview.

I will record operational notes under an anonymized participant ID. Your name and organization will not appear in the research summary unless you separately approve attribution.

## Recording consent

Would you like this session to remain notes-only, or do you consent to audio/screen recording for research review?

- [ ] Notes only
- [ ] Audio recording approved
- [ ] Screen recording approved
- [ ] Recording declined

A recording refusal does not affect participation.

## Source-document confirmation

- [ ] No source document viewed
- [ ] Synthetic/redacted document viewed
- [ ] Permissioned real document viewed inside participant environment
- [ ] Source document copied or retained — **must normally remain unchecked**

## Closing confirmation

May I contact you to verify the workflow summary or invite your team to a supervised pilot later?

- [ ] Follow-up clarification approved
- [ ] Pilot follow-up approved
- [ ] No follow-up
