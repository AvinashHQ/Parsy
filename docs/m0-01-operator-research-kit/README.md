# M0-01 Operator Interviews and Workflow Baseline

## Objective

Understand the current invoice-processing workflow well enough to define the first live MVP around measured operator pain rather than assumptions.

The issue is complete when the evidence package contains at least five completed operator interviews and a comparable workflow baseline for each participant, including touch time and error categories.

## Minimum acceptance criteria

1. At least five completed interviews with people who personally perform or review invoice entry.
2. Every interview has a completed note record and workflow map.
3. Baseline minutes are recorded for each interview as either:
   - directly observed timing; or
   - reconstructed timing supported by a recent batch, system timestamps, or operator records.
4. Error categories and unacceptable-error examples are recorded.
5. Findings are synthesized into changes or confirmations for:
   - `docs/01_PRODUCT_BRIEF.md`; and
   - `docs/09_BUSINESS_VALIDATION.md`.
6. Sensitive source documents are not placed in interview notes, spreadsheets, issue comments, recordings, or general-purpose cloud drives.

## Recommended quality bar

The following exceeds the literal acceptance criteria but is recommended before closing the issue:

- Five interviews across at least three organizations.
- At least three hands-on operators and one reviewer/team lead.
- At least two live workflow observations.
- At least one team processing multi-country or multi-currency invoices.
- At least one spreadsheet-first workflow and one accounting-system import workflow.
- Baseline based on at least 20 documents in aggregate, not only memory-based estimates.
