They are suitable for:

- freezing an internal MVP scope;
- designing Milestone M1;
- creating issue acceptance criteria and fixtures; and
- illustrating the evidence that a real M0 must produce.

They are not suitable for:

- marketing claims;
- investor or customer evidence;
- privacy or legal attestations;
- production launch approval; or
- representing that agreements or interviews actually occurred.

Before an external pilot, replace the assumed records with signed terms, permissioned documents, actual measurements, and named internal approvals.
