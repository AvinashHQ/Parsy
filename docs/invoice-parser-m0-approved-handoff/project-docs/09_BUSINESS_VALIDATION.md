# Business Validation — M0 Results

> Synthetic planning output; replace with actual interview evidence before external use.

## Result

M0 supports a **GO** decision for the supervised reviewed-export MVP.

## Evidence

- 7 qualifying interviews
- 5 organizations
- 3 observed workflows
- 144 documents represented in timing samples
- 6/7 found reviewed XLSX/CSV useful
- 5/7 were willing to test an export-only workflow
- 2 pilot profiles selected

## Baseline

- mixed median touch time: 5.6 min/document
- header/totals median: 4.2 min/document
- line-item median: 9.1 min/document
- mean rework: 1.4 min/document
- 29% of documents had at least one exception
- 8% required external clarification

## Hypothesis decisions

| Hypothesis | Decision |
|---|---|
| Review time is the dominant adoption metric | Confirmed |
| Generic reviewed exports create pre-integration value | Confirmed |
| Arithmetic/evidence warnings improve trust | Confirmed |
| First paid expansion is one region or ERP mapping | Mixed; defer selection |
| Short explicit retention is acceptable | Confirmed for supervised pilot |

## Commercial wedge

Sell reduced operator time per approved document and faster batch completion. Do not market a universal AI parser or tax-compliance engine.

## Pilot offer

- supervised invite-only workspace;
- 50–100 document batches;
- named fields/export profile;
- operator approval required;
- accuracy, review time, cost and exceptions reported;
- no direct posting.

## Go conditions carried into M5

- ≥50% batch time reduction;
- auto-accepted high-risk precision ≥99.5% if auto-accept is later enabled;
- zero escaped critical monetary inconsistencies;
- acceptable machine plus support cost;
- at least one team requests continued access.

## Decision

Start M1 with no ERP adapter, regional compliance pack or model provider dependency.
